WHACKABALL
Whackaball is a multiplayer ROBLOX game where you utilize ball-rolling mechanics and your goal is to knock as many
players off the platform. The game includes multiple maps that I have designed and programmed on my own, and it also
includes a game round system and a map changing system. Developing this game has given me first-hand experience
in developing round based games, ball-rolling physics, and map systems.
